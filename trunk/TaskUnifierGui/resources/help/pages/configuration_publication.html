Publication

Publish to:
TaskUnifier can publish your tasks to different third-party websites or applications.
It is a one-way publication, which means your data from TaskUnifier will be sent to the third-party website or application but if you change something in the third-party website or application, these changes won't be replicated in TaskUnifier.
Install new publisher plugins:
Install new plugins to publish your tasks to different third-party websites or applications.
Plugins:
In the table, you can select which plugin must be enabled and configure their settings.
